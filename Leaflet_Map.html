--- title: "Leaflet map" author: "John Andoh" date: "7/19/2020" output: html_document --- ```{r setup, include=FALSE} knitr::opts_chunk$set(echo = TRUE) ``` ## RMarkdown Leaflet assignment ###Some Universities in Ghana ```{r, echo=F} library(dplyr) library(leaflet) Uni<-data.frame(name = c("University of Ghana","Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology","Ashesi University","University of Cape Coast","University of Education","Valley View University"), pop = c(40000,56000,1200,6000,46000,15000), lat =c(5.650562,6.674504,5.759800,5.103506,5.4415677,5.799337), lng = c(-0.1962244,-1.5716114,-0.2197507,-1.2824830,-0.9529959,-0.1260682), status =c("Blue","Blue","Yellow","Blue","Blue","Yellow"), stringsAsFactors = F) Uni%>% leaflet()%>% addTiles()%>% addCircleMarkers(label = Uni$name, color = Uni$status)%>% addCircles(weight = 1, radius=sqrt(Uni$pop)*40)%>% addLegend(labels = c("Public","Private"), colors = c("blue", "yellow")) ```
